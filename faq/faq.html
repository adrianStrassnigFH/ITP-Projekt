FH SPIELWELT FAQ

1. How do I play the games?
Simply click on any game from the main menu to start playing. Each game has its own controls which will be explained when you launch it.
2. What is the leaderboard?
The leaderboard tracks high scores from all players. Compete with friends to see who can get the highest score in each game!
3. Are there any system requirements?
Our games are designed to work on most modern web browsers. For best performance, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
4. Can I save my game progress?
Yes! Your high scores are automatically saved in your browser's local storage. For permanent saving, we recommend creating an account (coming soon).
5. Is this platform free to use?
Yes, FH SPIELWELT is completely free to play with no hidden costs or microtransactions.
